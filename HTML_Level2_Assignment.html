  1. is video tag a semantic tag
Answer: No, the video tag is not a semantic tag.Semantic tags, like header, article, section, and footer, give meaning to the content they contain, helping search engines and developers understand the page structure. The video tag, on the other hand, is a media element used to embed videos in a webpage.
